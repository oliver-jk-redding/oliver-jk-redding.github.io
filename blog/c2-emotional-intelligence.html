What learnings did I take from the chapter?

12 December 2015

I learned that one can train oneself to achieve more positive states of mind and that such states can affect one's leadership abilities and performance. I learned that through physiological actions one can one's psychological self.

How did I find the two minutes of mindfulness?

12 December 2015

I found that I was able to slow down my heart rate and achieve a greater sense of calm. I tried the 'easier' approach by just relaxing without an agenda. When I finished, I was able to return to my task with a greater sense of focus. I think for best results, this could be combined with a brief walk outside to add fresh air to the activity.
