Daily Mindfulness

16 January 2016

What did I learn from the 'mindfulness conversation'? How did the other person find it?

The mindfulness conversation is a timely strategy for the planning of my wedding. Planning for such an event involves many conversations often under a high level of stress. I was able to adopt this strategy for one of these conversations and it enabled me to really listen to and try to understand my partner. The conversation resulted in greater clarity and agreement, and less argument and tension. My partner found the approach considerate, and she enjoyed being given full attention and feeling listened to and understood. I believe mindful conversation can be used to inject greater appreciation of each other into the relationship. It is a very promising tool.

Which of the three suggested techniques will I use to sustain my practice?

I am keen to introduce my partner to mindulness conversation and practice with her as I see this as a potential tool to foster better communication and closeness in our relationship. I also plan to try taking one mindful breath a day as I am terrible at making time to do meditation but this at least seems achievable and sustainable.
